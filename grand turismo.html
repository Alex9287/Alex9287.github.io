Overview

Gran Turismo is a high-fidelity racing game by Polyphony Digital. Experience life-like driving physics, real cars, and stunning visuals across world-famous tracks. Whether you’re a casual driver or hardcore racing fan, this game delivers the ultimate sim experience.

Key Features

  • Over 400 real-world cars
  • World circuits from Tokyo, Nürburgring and more
  • Photo-realistic lighting and visuals
  • Car tuning, licensing, and career modes
  • Online racing and time trials
