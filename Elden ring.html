Overview

Elden Ring is a massive open-world action RPG developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco. It features a rich lore created in collaboration with fantasy author George R.R. Martin, and offers intense combat, stunning landscapes, and deep exploration.

Key Features

  • Massive open world with dynamic weather and day-night cycles
  • Character customization with dozens of weapons and spells
  • Epic boss fights with stunning design and challenge
  • Deep lore and hidden secrets
  • Multiplayer co-op and PvP options
