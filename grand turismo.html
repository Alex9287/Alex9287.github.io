Info: Gran Turismo is a realistic racing simulation series developed by Polyphony Digital. It features real-world cars, tracks, and driving physics that appeal to motorsport fans and gamers alike.

Game Rating: 4 and a half stars

Key Features

  • Stunningly realistic car models and environments
  • Large selection of real-world vehicles
  • Detailed tuning, upgrades, and customization
  • Single-player campaigns and online multiplayer
  • VR support in latest versions (PS5)

Gameplay Tip

Use racing lines and braking zones to improve lap times. Practice with time trials to master tracks.

Achievements

  • Completed all circuit experiences
